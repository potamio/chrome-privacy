Privacy Policy for Tab Grid

Last updated: [2025-01-23]

Information Collection and Use

Quick Tab Navigator is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be completely transparent about what information the extension handles:

  • The extension accesses URLs and thumbnails of your open tabs to display them in the navigation grid.
  • All data processing occurs locally in your browser.
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted to external servers.
  • User preferences (such as custom shortcuts) are stored locally using browser storage.

Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you configure are stored locally in your browser using the built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

Data Security

Since all data processing occurs locally within your browser and no data is transmitted externally, your information remains secure on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
